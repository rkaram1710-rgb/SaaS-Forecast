High-level metrics and charts formatted for immediate presentation to your board, advisors, and investors.

Do I need a finance degree to use this?
No. The model separates operational inputs from complex accounting. If you know your business metrics, you can confidently use this model.
Is this built specifically for SaaS?
Yes. It is deeply optimized for recurring revenue mechanics, accurately tracking MRR, ARR, Churn, LTV, and CAC.
Can I edit the assumptions?
Absolutely. It is fully driver-based. Change pricing, hiring plans, or churn rates, and see the impact flow through all statements instantly.
What format is the product delivered in?
It is delivered as an Excel file. Download it immediately after purchase and use it offline at your own pace.
